MarketVue

This project is meant to be a testbed for the development of different tools that could help us analyze markets and financial fluxes. This could facilitate carrying out further analysis (automated or computer-aided) and valuation of companies, assets, and sectors.

This web app is under construction. At the moment, I am exploring different ways of visualizing said data, using graph networks. The next step would be to implement the querying mechanisms, followed by valuation models.
